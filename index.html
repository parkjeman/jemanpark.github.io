Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Central Florida, Orlando, in 2020, and Bachelor at Korea University, in Seoul, South Korea, in 2016.

At Georgia Tech, I am a member of the CyberForensics Innovation Lab, and advised by Prof. Brendan Saltaformaggio. I am intested in the wide scope of computer and network security, especially Cyber Forensics, Malware Analysis, DNS system, and DDoS detection.

More about me can be found in my CV.

Research

Domain Name System (DNS)

The distribution and behavior of open resolvers over the world.

Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)

Detection, evaluation, and mitigation of DDoS attack.
